SELECT-GCA (NEJM 2025) GCA

Upadacitinib 15mg superior to placebo (46.4% vs 29.0%, p=0.002). 7.5mg NOT superior. No MACE. N=428.

‚Üí ACR Abstract  |  ‚Üí NEJM Publication

METOGiA Trial (ACR 2025) GCA

MTX vs TCZ: MTX 37% vs TCZ 46% sustained remission at week 78. MTX NOT non-inferior (p=0.054). N=218. Safety concern: 5 Pneumocystis infections with MTX (1 fatal).

‚Üí ACR Abstract

MANDARA (NEJM 2024) EGPA

Benralizumab non-inferior to mepolizumab (58% vs 56%). Fewer serious AEs (6% vs 13%). N=140.

‚Üí NEJM Publication

MITIGATE Trial (ACR 2025) IgG4-RD

Inebilizumab (CD19 B-cell depleting mAb): 87% reduction in flare risk vs placebo. Phase 3 data. N=135.
